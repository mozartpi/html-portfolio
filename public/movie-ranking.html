The Best Movies According to Mozart

My top 3 movies of all time.

Back to the Future

Favourite of all time. Sci-Fi, comedy and some drama, for an eleven years old boy, it was really perfect!!

King Richard

A movie that tells you to go forward, believe in your dreams, find the help you need and do not give up!

Spider-Man (Sam Raimi)

A great superheroe film. At my late 20's, it was great to see one of my favourite characters on live action, in a great story!
